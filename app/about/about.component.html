About me

Welcome to my blog and thanks for taking the time to read this.

My name is Alan Beech, I live in the South West of the UK and I’ve been a software developer for 19 years since leaving university where I studied computer science. For 16 of those 19 years I’ve worked as a freelance/contract developer in large enterprises mainly using Microsoft technologies to produce web and in more recent years mobile applications for both internal and external consumption.

My journey into software development started long before my university days when a Sinclair ZX81 was brought into my secondary school in the early 80’s. Before long every school break time was spent writing programs on the ZX81, Spectrum and BBC Micro… I was hooked.

I spend a huge amount of time outside my normal working hours trying to perfect my software development skills watching training videos, reading blogs and attending and helping out at developer conferences. I’m also usually working on a number of side projects which enables me to solidify my learning whilst working with the latest technologies which often take their time to get to the enterprise.

Hopefully my posts here will help people out in the way that the blogs of others have helped me learn and understand things over the years.

Thanks again for reading.

Alan Beech
