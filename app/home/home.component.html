Xamarin Forms - Detect Swipe - Friday 22nd April 2016

In this blog post I detail the steps involved in detecting left/right/up/down swipes on an image in Xamarin Forms something at the time of writing this post is not built in to the Xamarin Forms abstractions. I use custom renderers creating a 'SwipeableImage' component in order to achieve this which adds Swipe events to the standard Image control.
